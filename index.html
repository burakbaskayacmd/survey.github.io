The highlight is the X-slayskin, a skeletal structured upper with the Adidas speed mesh which allows you to make deft touches on the ball and deliver speedy, powerful shots into the back of the net. Adidas Predator 20.3 LL

This is an improved version of Adidas Predator 20.3 IN, in which a slightly different design and set of technologies, borrowed in part from the top version. Let's list the main chips of this model: Seamless Purecut Technology top coat from Controlskin; elastic Sockfit panel instead of lacing; cushioning under the EVA heel. This version is more suitable for outdoor games, but also suitable for playing in the hall for those who want a futsal without laces. It feels like they are a cross between the Predator 20.3 and the Predator Mutator 20+, where there is more advantage in favor of a cheap variation.
